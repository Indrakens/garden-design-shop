We start with an initial light hearted yet serious site visit (On-Site Consultation) at your home. To see your home, it's setting, house style and a chance to walk the site and look at various conceptual garden design options available for a zone or the whole site.

Step 1:  The Site Survey and Client design brief

If the client is happy wit the initial OSC they may instruct the designer to proceed withthe site survey & client garden design brief.

Step 2:  The Conceptual Garden Design Package option

This is a very cost effective way of getting a zonal or holistic conceptual master plan, hard & soft mood boards, outline material and build costs without commiting to the full design package.

Step 3:  An optional 3D Conceptual sketch

A scaled 3D view of the garden zone or holistic.

Step 4:  Planting Plans and planting schedule

Clients can have a specific plant border designed or whole garden planting design.

Step 5:  Optional Construction details and setting out details drawing.

Detailed drawings of structures, walls, boundaries steps, water features etc.

Step 6:  Optional garden construction build specification and tender documents

Some clients are happy to go with their own landscaper but it's possible to get 2 or 3 contractors to quote for their new garde build and a set of client specific contract documents can be prepared and the tenders analysed for clients.

Step 7:  Garden build project supervision and Post build garden maintenance

The garden designer is happy to supervise the garden project for clients on a visit by visit fee basis and a Post build garden maintenance schedule can also be prepared.
